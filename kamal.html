Kamal Haasan[b] (born 7 November 1954) is an Indian actor, filmmaker and politician who predominantly works in Tamil cinema, and currently serving as a Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha for Tamil Nadu.[6] Regarded as one of the greatest and most versatile actors of all time,[7] Haasan is renowned for portraying diverse and complex roles, across multiple genres, often involving physical transformations.[8] He is also known for pushing creative boundaries[9] and pioneering many new technologies in Indian cinema.[10]
